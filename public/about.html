About the Club

Founded in 2025, ColorStack at UTRGV proudly stands a chapter of the national ColorStack organization. Our mission is rooted deeply in the empowerment and support of Black, Latinx, and Indigenous students navigating the world of computing. ColorStack at UTRGV serves as a home and resource for those who are in tech majors such as Computer Science, Data Science, Information Science, and Computer Engineering.

Through our initiatives, we aim to increase the success of our members by facilitating access to internship opportunities, fellowships, and conferences such as Tapia, Grace Hopper, and AfroTech.
